Golfing

I have played golf for the past 27 years and was earned a collegiate athletic scholarship at University of Arkansas for 2 years before transferring to University of Oklahoma.

I have played from the island of Hawaii to the mountains of Colorado to the deserts of Nevada.

Golfing has taught me about the importance of teamwork, pushing past your physical and mental blocks, and never giving up.

Traveling

Traveling is one my major passions. When I was a child, my family often took me to the U.S. Virgin islands where the beaches were crystal clear. In high school, I made my first "solo" trip without my family and traveled around Europe (Switzerland, Italy, and France) for 2 weeks.

My favorite place to travel to is Japan. My fiance and I have travled her twice over the last 8 years. Our favorite thing to do is see new landmarks, eat delicious katsu and play at the arcade. On October 10th, 2024, my fiance proposed to me at the Hakone-jinja shrine in Hakone. Japan will always have my heart.

Thankfully, my fiance is also very passionate about weight lifting and he is able to assist me in providing suggestions, different exercises for strenthening, and overall motivation.

My Cat

My sweet siamese seal point cat is named Shinji. Shinji came into our lives when we needed him the most. We lost our 9 month old kitten Shadow in 2021, when he had a rare genetic heart anamoly that was triggered when he went under anesthesia for neutering. In 2022, my parents found Shinji online with a foster mother in Arkansas.

Two days later, my parents drove to Arkansas from Oklahooma, picked him up and then drove to Dalls so that I could pick him up at the airport. Since then, Shinji has been the light in our lives.

With his sweet snuggles, big beautiful blue eyes, and his playful curiousity he is always able to bring a smile to our faces.
